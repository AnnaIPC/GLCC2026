1. Synpolar cells

Transition video

On cooling from the isotropic phase through N and SmZA to NF, below 70°C a uniform texture with conic defects appears first. At around ~1°C below the transition, structural changes are observed, resulting in the formation of twist domain walls. These walls separate domains with oppositely twisted states, revealed by de-crossing of the polarizers.
