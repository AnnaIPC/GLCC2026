This page follows the structure of the poster and presents molecular alignment, twist evolution, and wedge-cell geometry using images and looping videos from different perspectives.

1. Synpolar cells

On cooling from the isotropic phase through N and SmZA to NF, below 70°C a uniform texture with conic defects appears first. At around ~1°C below the transition, we observe structural changes resulting in the formation of twist domain walls. The domain walls separate domains with oppositely twisted states (see Figure on poster) revealed by de-crossing of the polarizers.
